Common questions about joining

What is the format for references?

Please provide their contact information (Name, Email, and Phone Number).

Should a resume be submitted for key members only or the whole team?

Please submit resumes for all key principal members who will work on projects.

Are there immediate service needs?

Yes. Hypatia Collaborative needs HR, IT, and professional development services urgently.

How are vendors selected beyond applying?

Selection is based on experience, community alignment, and references. See the Evaluation Criteria in the RFP.

Is travel required to work with Hypatia Collaborative?

No mandatory travel. Remote work is welcome. Any travel would be discussed with individual clients.

How are vendors paid?

Invoices are typically paid within 30 days. Monthly invoicing is standard.

Can vendors submit for multiple services?

Yes, one combined proposal is fine for open RFPs. Separate submissions are needed for specialized services like IT, HR, or Accounting.

Full Vendor FAQ

How large is the current pool of vendors?

We currently have 13 vendors in our network and are looking to expand.

How long has this initiative been active?

The initiative started in February 2024 as a 3-year pilot funded by the City of St. Petersburg and Pinellas Community Foundation.

What is the process for project assignment?

Projects typically begin late June/early July. New cohorts launch every 3–4 months.

Who makes up the participants in the membership pool?

Currently these are nonprofit clients in St. Petersburg, with expansion plans to other areas and types of nonprofits.

Is there an established budget for services?

Budgets are determined by client needs. Standardized service costs apply.

Is there a pre-bid conference?

We are taking questions now and may host a pre-bid conference if needed.

Are public record redactions available?

Yes. We are happy to redact sensitive information upon request.

Is there a portal for daily ticketing (IT RFP)?

Possibly in the future for larger clients. Not currently active.

What are the pay terms after resolved ticket/invoice?

Invoices are paid within 30 days of submission.
